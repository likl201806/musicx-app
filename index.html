Overview

MusicX is a private cloud music & video player for iOS and Android. It helps you organize and play your own media library from local storage and supported cloud/self-hosted sources.

Key Features

Audio & video playback in a unified player screen
Local library management (including folders)
Wi-Fi transfer (local network)
Optional iCloud Drive storage for notes and bookmarks
Built-in browser (with user-initiated import actions)
Optional Last.fm login for scrobbling and music history
